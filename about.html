About Me

Hi, I’m Ashley — a 14‑year‑old content creator who thrives on blending creativity with technology. From designing and experimenting with 3D printing projects to building immersive fantasy worlds through writing, I love bringing ideas to life in both digital and physical forms.

As a storyteller, I expand chapters into rich, novel‑length scenes filled with layered characters and vivid detail. As a maker, I enjoy formatting manuscripts with professional polish and designing websites that reflect a clean, modern aesthetic.

Music is another cornerstone of my creativity — diving into rock and alternative trivia, following powerful vocalists, and exploring the history of bands and collaborations fuels my inspiration.

Curious, playful, and driven, I’m always learning new skills and finding fresh ways to share my work. Whether through writing, design, or hands‑on projects, I’m building a creative journey that’s just getting started.
